Biomedical Research

Abhi has created an algorithm for recording and analyzing gastrointestinal sounds in real-time. He used a custom bandpass filter and Bayesian Wavelet Denoising to filter the sounds directly recorded from a microphone on the abdomen, before designing a movel algorithm to estimate the amount of activity in the gastrointestinal tract at a given time. One application of this project was to detect necrotizing enterocolitis, a devastating disease that occurs in infants with a fatality rate of nearly 30%. One sign of necrotizing enterocolitis is absent bowel sounds, which could diagnosed by the algorithm. The research was published in an article in a peer-reviewed journal here.

Bus Route Efficiency

Abhi has co-founded a startup that uses software to optimize bus routes. He has developed a ecosystem that includes a web interface for administrators, an iOS app for students and teachers, and a back-end database for calculations. The ecosystem optimizes bus routes by choosing a route based on the students who elect to go on the bus on any given day. Currently In Progress.

Modular Chess

Abhi has built a program that allows a user to play chess. The program can save and laod games to text files, allowing users to stop and continue their games at any given time. Furthermore, the program design supports inserting unique client-defined pieces into the gameboard. For example, a few edits would allow a client to insert a mystery-piece, whose moves could be similar to a horse, except with a 3x2 movement, instead of a 2x1.
